Introduction

gMetis is a pioneering AI-agent-driven crypto project built on the Metis Layer 2 of Ethereum. It combines the engaging aspects of a meme token with the utility of an AI-driven community management tool. The project aims to foster a vibrant and interactive community while providing real-time data and rewards to its members.

Vision and Mission

Our vision is to create a dynamic and engaging community where members can interact seamlessly with AI-driven tools. Our mission is to leverage the power of AI to enhance community interactions, provide valuable insights, and reward active participation.

Motto

"AI Agent - Meme token with a hint of utility in case the market moves that way."

Tokenomics

Token Type and Specifications

gMetis is an ERC-20 token adhering to the latest standards. It is designed to be simple and efficient, with no additional features such as minting, burning, or pausing capabilities after the initial mint.

Total Supply

The total supply of gMetis is 10,000,000 tokens.

Token Allocation

  • 30% (3,000,000 gMetis): Provided in the liquidity pool for public token sale on Hercules DEX (Metis mainnet, Andromeda). Liquidity will be locked for 2 years.
  • 36.5% (3,650,000 gMetis): Allocated for rewards.
  • 13.5% (1,350,000 gMetis): Team budget.
  • 10% (1,000,000 gMetis): Infrastructure costs.
  • 10% (1,000,000 gMetis): Marketing.

Budget Allocation

The budget is allocated to ensure the smooth operation and growth of the project:

  • Team: 13.5% of the total supply.
  • Infrastructure: 10% of the total supply.
  • Marketing: 10% of the total supply.

Utility

gMetis tokens have a unique utility within the project. The AI agent uses the tokens to reward active community members. The reward amount is dynamically calculated based on the user's token holdings, incentivizing users to hold more tokens to receive higher rewards.

Initial Mint and Contract Renouncement

All tokens will be minted at launch. The contract will be renounced post-launch, ensuring no further minting or modifications can be made.

Liquidity Pool Details

The liquidity pool will be established on Hercules DEX on the Metis mainnet (Andromeda). Liquidity will be locked for a period of 2 years to ensure stability and trust.

Reward System

Reward Distribution Process

The AI agent manages the reward distribution process. Each day, it randomly selects one member from the top 5-10 interactors based on their engagement levels. The selected member is contacted via private message to provide their wallet address.

Steps:

  1. Winner Selection: The AI agent selects a winner based on engagement metrics. One user can win maximum once per week.
  2. Announcement: The AI agent announces the winner in the main Telegram group chat.

    Example message: "Congratulations @username! You have been selected as today's top contributor. Please check your private messages to provide your wallet address for the reward."

  3. Private Message: The AI agent sends a private message to the winner requesting their wallet address.

    Example private message: "Hi @username, congratulations on being selected as today's top contributor! Please reply with your wallet address to receive your reward."

  4. Wallet Address Submission: The winner responds with their wallet address.

    Example response: "My wallet address is 0x1234...5678."

  5. Verification: The AI agent verifies the wallet address format.

    Example message: "Thank you, @username. Your wallet address has been received and verified. Your reward will be processed shortly."

  6. Token Holding Verification: The AI agent checks the winner's wallet on the blockchain to verify the amount of gMetis tokens held.
  7. Reward Calculation: Based on the token holdings, the AI agent calculates the reward amount using the following logic:
    • If the user holds 10,000 gMetis tokens, they receive 100% of the reward (5,000 tokens).
    • If the user holds no tokens, they receive 20% of the reward (1,000 tokens).
    • For other amounts, the reward \( A \) is calculated as: 
      A = 5000 × (0.2 + (H / 10000) × 0.8)
      where \( H \) is the number of gMetis tokens held by the user.
  8. Reward Distribution: The AI agent initiates an on-chain transaction to send the calculated reward to the winner's wallet.
  9. Public Ledger Update: The AI agent updates the public ledger or the reward distribution section on the website.

Criteria for Selecting Winners

Winners are chosen based on their interaction metrics, such as the number of messages and the quality of their contributions. The AI ensures the selection process is fair and transparent.

Reward Allocation and Distribution

  • Reward Wallet: 36.5% of the total gMetis supply is allocated to a separate wallet controlled by the AI agent.
  • Reward Amount: Each reward is dynamically calculated based on the user's token holdings.
  • Distribution Method: The AI agent sends the reward tokens to the winner's wallet via an on-chain transaction. The distribution is reflected in the project's database and displayed on the website.

Technical Overview

Smart Contract Development

The gMetis ERC-20 token contract will be developed according to the latest Ethereum standards to ensure security, efficiency, and compatibility. The contract will include the following features:

  • Initial Minting: All 10,000,000 gMetis tokens will be minted at the time of contract deployment. This ensures a fixed supply and prevents inflation.
  • Ownership Renouncement: After the initial minting, the contract ownership will be renounced. This means no further modifications or minting can occur, ensuring trust and transparency.
  • Standard ERC-20 Functions: The contract will implement all standard ERC-20 functions, including transfer, approve, and transferFrom, to facilitate token transactions and integrations with other platforms.
  • Security Measures: The contract will undergo rigorous security audits to identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities. This includes checks for reentrancy attacks, overflow/underflow issues, and other common smart contract risks.

AI Backend Development

The AI agent's backend will be a critical component of the gMetis project, enabling advanced community management and interaction capabilities. The development will focus on the following areas:

  • Community Interaction: The AI agent will be designed to read and respond to conversations on Telegram and X.com, initiate new discussions, and maintain continuous engagement. It will act as a community moderator, ensuring discussions remain relevant and positive.
  • Sentiment Analysis: The AI will perform sentiment analysis on user interactions to gauge the overall mood and engagement level of the community. This data will be used to make informed decisions and improve community management.
  • Moderation Capabilities: Advanced algorithms will be implemented to detect and block inappropriate content, including offensive language, spam, and irrelevant posts. This ensures a high-quality interaction environment.
  • Reward Distribution: The AI agent will manage the reward distribution process, selecting winners based on engagement metrics, verifying token holdings, and calculating rewards. It will initiate on-chain transactions to distribute rewards and update the public ledger.
  • Integration with APIs: The AI backend will integrate with Telegram and X.com APIs to enable seamless interaction and data exchange. It will also fetch real-time data on cryptocurrency prices and hot conversations to provide timely updates to the community.
  • Security and Scalability: The backend will be developed with a focus on security and scalability, ensuring it can handle a growing number of users and interactions without compromising performance or data integrity.
